Spacial Memory Project

This summer the YHD are conducting a spatial memory project. Students will select businesses and empty lots on primarily 24th, and then 16th and 30th Streets, observe what is currently there and research its history. The final part of the project asks students to reimagine spaces and discuss what could or should be in those spaces to benefit the community.
